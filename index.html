  • Ellites (Elliot's team) — Lowest ceiling in the league: no hitter above .724, and now only five players clearing .500. No stars, no chance — though Larry Stubbs (R7, .640) is quietly a real find.
  • Playas (Michael's team) — Their real opponent is attendance: 258 team at-bats vs. the Pliggas' 383. Their best bat, Nathan Knudson (.733), has 30 at-bats all season — and their captain is round 1's underperformer (see Outliers).
